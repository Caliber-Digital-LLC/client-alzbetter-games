How to Play

Goal

Transform words by changing one letter at a time. Keep changing words to stay alive and beat your high score!

How to Play

  • Choose a category: 3, 4, 5, or 6 letter words
  • Drag letters from the bottom to replace letters in the word
  • Form valid new words before time runs out
  • Each word you create extends your time

Power-Ups

  • Hint: Shows one letter of a possible word
  • Full Hint: Reveals an entire possible word
  • Reset: Gets a completely new starting word

Tips

  • Start with shorter words to learn the mechanics
