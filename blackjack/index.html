How to Play

Goal

Get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over.

Card Values

  • Number cards (2-10): Face value
  • Face cards (J, Q, K): 10 points
  • Aces: 11 or 1 (whichever helps most)

Rules

  • Dealer stands on 17 and above
  • Blackjack (21 on first two cards) pays 3:2
  • Double Down: Double your bet for one more card

How to Play

  • Tap chips to place your bet
  • Tap DEAL to start the hand
  • Choose HIT, STAND, or DOUBLE
